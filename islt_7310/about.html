Spencer Smith

My name is Spencer Smith and I am a teacher and coach for the state of Missouri. I grew up in and graduated high school from a small, rural southwest Iowa farm town, and attended Northwest Missouri State University in Maryville, MO. I graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Physical Education, and since graduation, I have been teaching elementary Physical Education and coaching in the Kansas City metropolitan area. During my first two years of teaching, I obtained my Master’s Degree in Educational Leadership, also from Northwest Missouri State University.
I have passion for helping others succeed, and Physical Education is the best way for me to do so.

Favorite Quote

My favorite quote is from Rita Pierson, and she said these powerful words:

“Every child deserves a champion—an adult who will never give up on them, who understands the power of connection and insists that they become the best that they can possibly be.”

Some days it is really tough, by I try to be champion for at least one student each day.
